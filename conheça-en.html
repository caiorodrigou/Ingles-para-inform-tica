About our academy

Performance is more than just a gym, it's a space dedicated to your well-being, health and evolution. Our mission is to provide a welcoming environment with modern facilities and high-quality equipment so you can achieve your goals, whether weight loss, muscle gain or physical fitness. We have a team of qualified professionals, ready to guide and motivate each student on their fitness journey. In addition to weightlifting and cardio areas, we offer dynamic group classes such as functional training, spinning, pilates and martial arts, ensuring variety to meet all profiles. At Performance, we believe that each workout is an opportunity to surpass yourself. Come be part of this community and discover the power of consistency and dedication!

Our Professional Team: Competence and Dedication

At Performance, we believe that our students' success is directly linked to the quality of the professionals who support them. That's why we have a highly qualified team passionate about what they do. Our trainers, personal trainers and instructors are certified and have experience in the fitness market, always updated with the best practices to offer efficient, safe and personalized training. Each member of our team is committed to helping you achieve your goals, whether weight loss, muscle gain, physical fitness or well-being. In addition to technical knowledge, our professionals offer humanized service, focused on motivation and individual support so that each student feels welcomed and confident in their journey. Beyond trainers, we have specialists in nutrition and physiotherapy, who assist in developing a complete plan for your evolution, ensuring that each workout is efficient and tailored to your needs.

Technology and Innovation at Performance

At Performance, we believe that technology can be a great ally in the pursuit of better results. That's why we constantly invest in innovation to provide the best experience for our students. Our gym has modern and state-of-the-art equipment, which guarantees more efficient, safe and adaptable training to different fitness levels. Additionally, we use intelligent systems for performance monitoring, allowing you to track your progress over time and adjust your workout routine strategically. Another advantage of Performance is our exclusive app, which facilitates access to personalized workouts, scheduling group classes and nutritional monitoring. With it, you can plan your exercise routine from anywhere and have the support you need in the palm of your hand. Here, we combine science, technology and expertise to provide an innovative and efficient environment for your progress. Come train with us and experience a new way to achieve your goals!
